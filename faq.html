Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to the FAQ Board! Here, we will answer the most common questions we receive.

What is Arngren Redesigned?

Arngren Redesigned is a makeover for the infamous Norwegian e-commerce website, Arngren.net, to make it more user-friendly and atheistically pleasing. You can't actually buy anything from here, but it provides a glimpse of what a useable version of Arngren may look like. Plus, it's in English, which makes it more accessable to a lot more of the world.

Why Arngren in the first place?

Good question! The main reason we chose Arngren is because of how awful it was. From having to scroll left to right to the overuse of bright colors, low-resolution images and a dated apperence, on top of having non-functional features, such as the search bar, we felt like we had to redesign the page.

How did we find Arngren?

Well, we couldn't think of a website to remake off the top of our heads, so we just used Google and searched something along the lines of "ugly websites", and we went from there!

What does our site do?

Our website, although not a functional storefront, does provide a working outline on how Arngren could look if the owner of the site decided to give the website a glow-up. Although if they actually went to redesign their page, other people won't be able to use Arngren for this class's final....

What grade will we get for this project?

Easy! a one billion percent!
